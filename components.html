"Be inspired by the heroic examples of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) and the righteous companions, and strive to embody their noble traits in your daily life."

"Whoever guides someone to goodness will have a reward equal to it." - Sahih Muslim 1893

This hadith emphasizes the importance of helping and guiding others towards goodness and righteousness, as the person who does so will be rewarded by Allah for their efforts. It is a reminder that our actions can have a positive impact on others and that we should strive to be a source of good in the world.
